Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
1. How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Fill in the required fields, including your name, email address, and password. Once you've submitted the form, check your email for a confirmation link to activate your account.
2. How do I reset my password?
If you’ve forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we’ll send you a link to create a new password.
3. How can I find hospitals near me?
To find hospitals near you, click on the "Hospitals Near Me" button from your account dashboard. Ensure that your location services are enabled, and we’ll provide a list of nearby hospitals based on your current location.
4. How do I update my profile information?
To update your profile information, log in to your account and navigate to the "Profile" section. Here, you can edit your name, contact information, and any other personal details.
5. How can I contact customer support?

6. What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?
If you experience any technical issues while using our website or app, please try clearing your browser cache or updating to the latest version of the app. If the problem persists, contact our support team with details of the issue, and we’ll assist you.
